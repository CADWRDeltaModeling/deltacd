deltacd.dcd.adjust_leach_water(da_lwa, da_lwd, da_ro)¶

Adjust (Reduce) leach water with runoff

When there is runoff from precipitation, applied leach water can be reduced by the amount of the runoff. Even more, the leftover of the runoff can be applied as leach water for following days, if available. This logic is adopted from the previous Fortran version of DCD with some modification.

Parameters:
da_lwa: xarray.DataArray

DataArray of daily applied leach water of all areas

da_lwd: xarray.DataArray

DataArray of daily drained leach water of all areas

da_ro: xarray.DataArray

DataArray of daily runoff of all areas

Returns:
xarray.DataArray, xarray.DataArray

DataArray of adjusted applied and drained leach water

Read an island-to-node distribution file and create a distribution matrix.

This function read a text file containing list of island-to-node distribution values and create a xarray.DataArray with a dense matrix size of (# of islands, # of distributed nodes). The coordinates of the DataArray are one-based indices of the islands and nodes.
